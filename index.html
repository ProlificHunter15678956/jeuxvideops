Two Ships At Night

Two ships at night is a game made during the js13k competion in 2021. It's an fps game made with webGL. The player must kill enemies after enemies to top their highscore. Two ships at night was made by github user razh.

SpaceWord

Spaceword is a game made during the js13k competition in 2021. The player controls with their mouse a cursor. As time goes on more asteroids are spawned. If the cursor touches one the player loses a life. They only have 3 in total. The goal is to keep going as long as possible. The game features collisions between asteroids. A fun quirk of the physics occurs when an asteroid spawns on top of another, one gains a lot of velocity.
